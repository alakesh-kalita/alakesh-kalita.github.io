Projects

Resilience in Low Power and Lossy Internet of Things Networks

Sponsor: IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, India.

Scheme: Faculty Research Scheme

Duration: October 2024 - October 2026

Role: Principal Investigator

Project Objectives

The primary goal of this project is to investigate and design security schemes for various data and network protocols in 6TiSCH IoT technology to counter cyber attacks in industrial IoT applications. In many cases, IoT protocols lack integrated security measures during control message exchange, leading to vulnerabilities across different layers.

Insecure control message exchange can serve as an exploitable point, potentially leading to cyber attacks. To mitigate this risk, it is crucial to incorporate security elements into the design of each protocol within 6TiSCH networks.
