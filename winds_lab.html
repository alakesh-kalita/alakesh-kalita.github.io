About the Lab

WiNDS Lab at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad focuses on exploring IoT (Internet of Things) and distributed systems (traditional computer networks) by practically implementing solutions to real-world problems. The lab is dedicated to improving the performance, scalability, and efficiency of existing IoT networks and distributed systems. By leveraging cutting-edge AI/ML technologies, the lab aims to revolutionize IoT and distributed systems, addressing critical challenges and driving innovation across multiple application domains.
