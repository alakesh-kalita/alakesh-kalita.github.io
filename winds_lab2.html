About the Lab

Welcome to the Wireless Networks and Distributed Systems Lab. Our lab focuses on cutting-edge research in wireless communication, IoT, and distributed computing.

Our research areas include:

  • 6TiSCH and LoRaWAN networks
  • Edge and Cloud computing
  • Task offloading and scheduling
  • AI/ML applications in network optimization

We collaborate with leading researchers and industries to develop innovative solutions for real-world problems.
